About Nexusone

Your trusted partner in IT asset and identity management

Who We Are

We provide a unified IT asset and identity management platform that tracks hardware, software, and licenses in real time from procurement to retirement. Our solution helps organizations manage employee onboarding and offboarding, control system access, maintain audit-ready records, reduce asset loss, and improve visibility, security, and cost efficiency across departments and locations.

From day one to day done, we manage the complete lifecycle of your IT assets and employee access. From procurement, configuration, and onboarding to tracking, support, recovery, and secure retirement, we ensure every device, license, and access right is properly assigned, monitored, and compliant—reducing risk while improving visibility and control.

Our Portfolio

We specialize in delivering secure and efficient employee onboarding and offboarding, robust Identity and Access Management (IAM), and comprehensive IT asset lifecycle operations.

Our services are supported by certified professionals with expertise in Okta, Microsoft Intune, and Google Workspace administration. Through this portfolio, we enable organizations to maintain compliance, optimize resource utilization, and strengthen security while ensuring seamless operational workflows.
