Our Services

Comprehensive IT asset and identity management solutions

Onboarding

Secure and streamlined onboarding process that prepares employee identities, system access, and devices from day one. New hires receive instant access to the tools they need through a single secure login, enabling them to start work without delays.

The platform automatically assigns the right applications, permissions, and device configurations based on each employee's role while protecting access with multi-factor authentication and identity management controls.

This efficient workflow strengthens security, reduces IT workload, and ensures a smooth onboarding experience for every new employee.

  • Role-based provisioning of apps, permissions, and devices on day one.
  • Single secure login with multi-factor authentication built in.
  • Instant access to the tools each new hire needs from the start.
  • Identity controls that protect access without slowing people down.

Offboarding

Secure and structured employee offboarding process that immediately disables all system access on the employee's final working day. All user accounts, applications, and permissions are promptly revoked to protect company data and prevent unauthorized access.

Company devices are securely collected, recorded, and tracked through a controlled asset management process. Each device then undergoes a data wiping procedure before being redeployed or reassigned.

This streamlined workflow protects sensitive information, maintains operational transparency, and ensures a secure transition for both HR and IT teams.

  • Instant access revocation across all accounts and applications.
  • Device recovery tracked through a controlled asset process.
  • Secure data wiping before redeployment or reassignment.
  • Full audit trail for transparent HR and IT handover.

Device Management

Comprehensive device management ensuring all company devices are securely configured, tracked, and maintained throughout their entire lifecycle.

Each device is registered, assigned to an employee, and monitored through a centralized management system, giving IT teams a single source of truth for every laptop, phone, and peripheral in the fleet.

Policy-based controls keep operating systems, security patches, and applications up to date automatically, while remote actions allow lost or compromised devices to be locked or wiped within minutes.

  • Zero-touch enrollment and automated configuration by employee role.
  • Policy enforcement for encryption, screen locks, and security baselines.
  • Automated patching of OS and application updates across the fleet.
  • Remote lock & wipe for lost, stolen, or compromised devices.

Warehouse & Inventory Management

Secure warehouse and inventory management ensuring company devices are properly stored, tracked, and maintained within a controlled environment.

Each device is logged, labeled, and inspected for condition and quality before being stored, redeployed, or shipped. The centralized inventory system provides clear visibility into asset location, availability, and status at all times.

This structured process ensures secure storage, organized asset tracking, and efficient access to inventory when devices are required.

  • Labeled & logged intake with condition and quality checks.
  • Centralized inventory with real-time location and status.
  • Ready-to-deploy stock available the moment devices are needed.
  • Controlled storage in a secure, organized environment.

Secure Data Wiping

Advanced secure data wiping services ensuring all sensitive client data is permanently removed from company devices before reuse, redeployment, or disposal.

The process follows certified industry data erasure standards to eliminate any possibility of data recovery. Each device undergoes a controlled wiping procedure with verification and detailed reporting to confirm complete data removal.

Every wipe generates a tamper-proof certificate of erasure, providing the documented evidence needed for compliance audits, chain-of-custody records, and data protection regulations such as GDPR and HIPAA.

  • Certified erasure aligned with NIST 800-88 and industry standards.
  • Certificate of destruction issued for every device wiped.
  • Safe redeployment of cleared devices back into the asset pool.
  • Compliance ready for GDPR, HIPAA, and internal audits.

Audit Logs

Comprehensive audit logs recording and monitoring all system activities, user access, and administrative actions across the platform.

Every login, access change, device activity, and system update is securely tracked to ensure full visibility and accountability.

Logs are time-stamped, immutable, and fully searchable, so IT and security teams can reconstruct exactly who did what and when — turning a stressful audit into a simple export.

  • Full traceability of every login, approval, and access change.
  • Immutable records that cannot be altered or deleted after the fact.
  • Fast search & filtering across users, devices, and date ranges.
  • One-click export for audits, reviews, and compliance reporting.
